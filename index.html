Welcome To The Foundation School!

The Foundation School established in the year 2003 as a symbol of educational aspiration for the students with a special purpose of providing quality education at an affordable price. We have never compromised on the quality of education and are still striving hard in upgrading and developing our classroom teaching practices and system.

Message

It is the contribution of our students to our school community that makes The Foundation School an exceptional learning community. Full participation in academic and co-curricular programs and a willingness to act responsibly as an individual within our educational environment are the factors that enable all to have a successful and enjoyable year

Salient Features:

  • The school management still ensures by practicing the policies and procedures:
    • The best quality of education with limited number of students.
    • The best learning opportunity of the students.
    • Individual attention on every student.
    • Striving for the best educational results.
  • Motivated, Passionate and enthusiastic results.
  • Stress on the development of positive attitude.
  • Fully equipped Science and Computer Laboratories.
  • Special emphasis on co-curricular activities.
  • Standby generator to provide relief to students during Load Shedding hours.
  • Regular Parent-Teacher meeting.
  • Inauguration of a new campus will be in 2019 and its building is designed and architected only for school.

Saying for Education

Primary

Established in 2003, The Foundation School (TFS) is an "Outstanding School" which prides itself on providing an excellent, broad and balanced curriculum and ensuring high standards in the core subject areas of Reading, Writing and Math. In our relentless drive for excellence, our aim is that all pupils should develop as excellent learners, have excellent social and emotional skills and fulfill their potential.

Secondary

We offer elementary education to children establishing foundations in range of subject areas. Through specially designed academic coursework, The Foudnation School aims to promote literacy and numeracy amongst the children. TFS ensures smooth transition to more advanced secondary education or middle school. Curriculum followed at Matric System is according to the Board of Secondary Education.
