History

The Foundation School waws exstablished in the year 2003 with dedication to provide quality education at an affordable cost to the play-group to Class III and with MOTTO "Brilliance Rises Above Outlay!"
To mould and refine the innate facilities of a child so as to grow noble cultured citizen. The best environment and facilities is prerequisite, hence, before inviting people for admission of their kids in the school, the premises was completed with Airy Class Rooms, Hygienic Environment, Comfortable Furniture and other articles of High Standard to help stimulate students interest in education.
Two students are never equal in caliber, i.e. keeping this thing in view and to offer the children with the opportunities to:

  1. Grow socially, emotionally, physically and intellectually.
  2. Develop communication and negotiation skills in relating other people.
  3. Learn to accept and appreciate differences among people.
  4. Make decisions and begin taking responsibility for those decisions.
  5. Improve problem solving skills.
  6. Gain divergent thinking skills as their own ideas and creativity.
  7. Most importantly, develop high self-esteem and self confidence.

The services of highly skilled and qualified faculty with professionalism were sought. The efforts brought remarkable results as tremendous interest was shown by the people of locality due to the reason, the up-gradation of school was felt.
At this juncture, it would not be out of place to mention that, this indeed is due to hard labor of our teaching faculty, brilliance of pupils and "Last but not the Least Supervision of Parents!"
The TFS System is build on an everlasting notion, "Enter to learn, go forth to serve the Nation". Today, when the nation requires bright and talented individuals to carry its torch of success, The Foundation of School is providing just the raw material required for the development. We at TFS believe that only "Hard work and Perseverance Guarantee Success."

Message

High standards and expectations for each student in regard to academic performance, co-curricular participation, and responsible citizenship are the foundation of our school. It is with pride that we hold these high standards and ask each of our students to commit to maintaining the extraordinary record of achievement and contribution that has been the legacy of The Foundation School students.
