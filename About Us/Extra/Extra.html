Advantages of Enhancement Activities:
  • Enhancement of self-esteem.
  • Development of the child's talents in arts or sport by becoming skilled at it.
  • Mental development.
  • They shed shyness or inferiority complex and learn to adjust well in a group.
  • Development of social skills like teamwork and coordination.
  • Develop better communication skills.
  • Besides improving school attendance and academic performance, the programs reinforce school learning with practical application.

The main benefit of enhancement activities programs is: It widens students' interests. It helps them discover innate talent for some activity and provides an opportunity to hone it further. Constant practice makes them more confident. In this age of endless possibilities, the students need portfolio skills besides academic learning to succeed.
